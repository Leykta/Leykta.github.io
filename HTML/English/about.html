Who are we??

We are Christian brothers who study and teach in the one, holy, universal and apostolic Orthodox Tewahedo Church founded by the love of God the Father, the blood of the Son and the Holy Spirit.

What will be the total content of this Website..??

We mainly focus on the faith and answer questions from Muslims and Protestants.. and if they allow us to do live discussions. In addition to this, it includes giving a series of basic dogma teachings and receiving questions from you Christians through Telegram, preparing short videos on them, and giving spiritual teachings related to growing up in a Christ-like life, etc.

Our purpose..??

Our main goal is to strengthen our Christian brothers and sisters in faith by making the Lord Jesus Christ their helper, so that they can understand their faith well and live in their faith so that they can reach out to others.

For you

We need all of you Christians who believe that this channel is useful to think of us in prayer and help us by visiting and sharing this website and spreading it to our other brothers and sisters.

May God protect the holy church.. Amen.
